# Blackstone Griddle Compatibility Skill

This skill analyzes recipe details to determine if a dish is suitable for cooking on an outdoor flat top griddle or Blackstone.

## Griddle-Friendly Culinary Indicators
*   **Stir-Fries & Hibachi**: Quick, high-heat seared protein and vegetable dishes.
*   **Smashed Burgers**: Thin beef patties smashed on hot iron surfaces.
*   **Seared Proteins**: Steaks, chicken thighs, fish fillets, pork chops requiring a hard crust.
*   **Mexican Grill**: Fajitas, quesadillas, street tacos, seared peppers/onions.
*   **Breakfast Griddles**: Pancakes, bacon/sausage, hash browns, scrambled eggs.
*   **Fried Rice & Noodles**: Stirred and flipped starches.

## Workflow
1. Read the recipe name and preparation instructions.
2. Verify if the ingredients and instructions match typical griddle techniques (sear, stir-fry, sauté on a flat plate).
3. If the recipe requires boiling, baking, roasting, deep-frying, or slow-cooking as the primary heat transfer method (e.g. stews, roasted chicken, cakes, soups), it is NOT compatible.
4. **Response**: Respond with ONLY "YES" or "NO". Do not add any conversational text or punctuation.
