# Weekly Themes Synopsis Skill

This skill analyzes a 7-day dinner menu and generates a brief, engaging culinary themes analysis.

## Guidelines
1.  **Identify Trends**: Pinpoint 1 or 2 prominent culinary trends, themes, styles, or repeating core ingredients in the upcoming week's dinners.
2.  **Style & Tone**:
    *   Sophisticated, engaging, and warm.
    *   Do NOT include greetings, names, emojis, or markdown formatting.
    *   Limit the response to under 60 words (typically 2-3 sentences).
